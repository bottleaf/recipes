3 cups of rice
4 Tbsp oil
4 green onion
5 eggs

1. Cook 3 cups of rice with 2.5 cups of water to get fresh dry rice.
2. Let rice cook by spreading on a baking sheet or just wait/stir
3. Cut green onions seperating green/white
4. Heat pan to high heat
5. Add oil and white part of green onion
6. after a few seconds add rice and stir
7. Push out an area of the pan and add eggs
8. After eggs look partiall done start stiring.
